About GDEH

Garissa Digital Empowerment Hub (GDEH) is a community-based organization committed to bridging the digital divide in Garissa County. The organization focuses on equipping youth, women, and the wider community with practical digital and entrepreneurial skills that improve livelihoods and promote self-reliance.

✖

About GDEH

GDEH was established in 2026 by a dedicated group of 10 founding members, including a Chairperson and Board Members. The founders shared a common vision of transforming Garissa into a digitally empowered community by providing accessible, affordable, and relevant training programs.

The organization operates with a strong commitment to community development, inclusivity, and innovation, ensuring that no one is left behind in the digital era.

Mission

To empower individuals in Garissa County with digital knowledge and practical skills for employment, entrepreneurship, and personal development.

Vision

To become a leading hub for digital empowerment and innovation in Northern Kenya.

Courses Offered

Smartphone Literacy - Basic phone use, internet, social media, mobile money.

Computer Literacy - Microsoft Office, typing, email, file management.

Agro-Entrepreneurship - Farming techniques, agribusiness, marketing.

ICT Short Courses - Graphic design, digital marketing, web basics.

Why Train With Us

Community-focused, affordable, practical training with mentorship.
